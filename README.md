# CalcTrust

**Accurate Salary and Tax Calculators You Can Trust**

A specialized financial reference platform providing precise salary conversions, paycheck estimates, and tax calculations for individuals across the United States. Built for transparency, accuracy, and ease of use.

---

## What CalcTrust Does

CalcTrust helps you answer critical financial questions instantly:

### For Job Seekers and Employees

- **Convert any salary format**: Wondering what your $75,000 annual salary means per hour? Or how that $35/hour rate translates to yearly income? Get instant, accurate conversions.

- **Estimate your take-home pay**: Enter your salary and see exactly how much lands in your bank account after federal taxes, state taxes, Social Security, and Medicare deductions.

- **Compare job offers**: Easily compare different salary structures (hourly vs. salaried, different states, various benefits) to make informed career decisions.

- **Plan salary negotiations**: Understand what gross salary you need to achieve your desired net income target.

### For Financial Planning

- **State-by-state tax comparisons**: See how your paycheck differs across all 50 states. Planning a move? Know exactly how state taxes will impact your income.

- **Overtime calculations**: Accurately calculate time-and-a-half and double-time pay for overtime hours.

- **401(k) contribution planning**: Understand how retirement contributions affect your take-home pay and plan your savings strategy.

- **Bonus tax estimates**: Know upfront how much of your bonus you'll actually receive after tax withholdings.

### Key Features

**274 Specialized Calculators**: Every calculator is purpose-built for a specific need, whether it's converting annual salary to hourly rate, estimating paychecks in California, or calculating freelance rates.

**State-Specific Accuracy**: Tax calculations account for each state's unique tax brackets, standard deductions, and local regulations. We cover all 50 states.

**Always Current**: Tax data is updated annually to reflect IRS changes, state tax adjustments, and FICA limits.

**Transparent Methodology**: Every calculation shows you exactly how we arrived at the numbers. No black boxes, no hidden assumptions.

**No Account Required**: Jump straight to answers. No signups, no personal data collection, no payment walls.

**Mobile-Friendly**: Access from any device, anywhere. Fast loading times and clean, readable results.

## Common Questions CalcTrust Answers

**"If I make $60,000 a year, what's my hourly rate?"**  
Instantly converts to $28.85 per hour (based on standard 40-hour work week).

**"How much will my paycheck be after taxes?"**  
Enter your salary and state, get your exact net pay including all deductions.

**"Should I take a $80k job in Texas or a $90k job in California?"**  
Compare take-home pay across states to make the best financial decision.

**"What gross salary do I need to take home $50,000?"**  
Calculates the required pre-tax income to achieve your net income goal.

**"How does my overtime pay work?"**  
Shows exactly how much you'll earn at time-and-a-half or double-time rates.
