## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much is $75,000 per hour?**
$75,000 ÷ 2,080 hours = $36.06 per hour.

**What is $75,000 biweekly after taxes?**
Approximately $2,150-2,400 depending on state, with no-tax states at the high end.

**Is $75K middle class?**
Yes. $75,000 is solidly middle class in most of America, comfortable in affordable areas.

**How much house can I afford on $75K?**
Using the 3x salary rule: ~$225,000 home. With 28% DTI rule: ~$1,750/month housing payment.
