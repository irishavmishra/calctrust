## How a 2% Raise Works

A 2% raise increases your salary by 2/100ths. On a $60,000 salary, a 2% raise means $1,200 more per year, or $100 extra per month.

## FAQ

**How much is a 2% raise on $60,000?**
2% of $60,000 = $1,200/year increase → New salary: $61,200

**Is a 2% raise average?**
2% is generally below the historical average raise of 3-4% but common in low-inflation environments or budget-constrained organizations.
