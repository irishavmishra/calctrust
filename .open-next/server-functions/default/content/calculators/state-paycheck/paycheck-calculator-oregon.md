## Deep Dive: Oregon Tax Structure

### Oregon Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 4.75% on income up to $4,050
- 6.75% on income $4,051 to $10,200
- 8.75% on income $10,201 to $125,000
- 9.9% on income over $125,000

### Oregon vs. Washington

Washington has no income tax. A $100,000 OR earner saves ~$7,500 annually in WA.

### But No Sales Tax

Oregon has no sales tax, partially offsetting the high income tax for some.
