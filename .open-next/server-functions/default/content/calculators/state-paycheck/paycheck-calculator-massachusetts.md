## Deep Dive: Massachusetts Tax Structure

### Massachusetts Flat Tax

Massachusetts imposes a flat 5% income tax on most income.

### Millionaire's Tax (2023+)

Income over $1,000,000 faces an additional 4% surcharge, for an effective 9% rate on income above the threshold.

### Massachusetts vs. New Hampshire

New Hampshire has no income tax on wages. Moving across the border saves 5% of income.
