## Deep Dive: Minnesota Tax Structure

### Minnesota Graduated Rates

**Single Filers (2025)**:
- 5.35% on income up to $31,690
- 6.8% on income $31,691 to $104,090
- 7.85% on income $104,091 to $183,340
- 9.85% on income over $183,340

Minnesota's 9.85% top rate is among the highest in the nation.
