## Deep Dive: Pennsylvania Tax Structure

### Pennsylvania Flat Tax

PA's 3.07% flat state rate is among the lowest in the nation.

### Local Earned Income Taxes

Most PA localities impose earned income taxes:
- Philadelphia: 3.75% city wage tax (residents), 3.44% (non-residents working in Philly)
- Pittsburgh: ~3% combined local
- Other localities: Typically 1-2%

### Combined Burden

PA state (3.07%) + Philadelphia (3.75%) = 6.82% combined, comparable to higher-rate states.

**What is Pennsylvania's income tax rate?**
