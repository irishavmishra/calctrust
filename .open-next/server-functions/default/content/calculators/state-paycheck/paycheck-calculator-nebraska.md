## Deep Dive: Nebraska Tax Structure

### Nebraska Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 2.46% on income up to $3,700
- 3.51% on income $3,701 to $22,170
- 5.01% on income $22,171 to $35,730
- 5.84% on income over $35,730

### Nebraska vs. Neighbors

South Dakota and Wyoming have no income tax. Iowa and Kansas have similar rates.
