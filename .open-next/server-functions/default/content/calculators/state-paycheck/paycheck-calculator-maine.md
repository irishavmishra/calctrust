## Deep Dive: Maine Tax Structure

### Maine Graduated Rates

**Single Filers (2025)**:
- 5.8% on income up to $24,500
- 6.75% on income $24,501 to $58,050
- 7.15% on income over $58,050

### Maine vs. New Hampshire

New Hampshire has no income tax on wages. A $75,000 earner saves ~$4,500 annually living in NH instead of Maine.
