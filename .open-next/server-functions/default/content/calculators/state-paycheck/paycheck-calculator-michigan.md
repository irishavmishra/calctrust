## Deep Dive: Michigan Tax Structure

### Michigan Flat Tax

Michigan imposes a flat 4.25% state income tax.

### City Income Taxes

Many Michigan cities impose local income taxes:

| City | Resident Rate | Non-Resident Rate |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| Detroit | 2.4% | 1.2% |
| Grand Rapids | 1.5% | 0.75% |
| Lansing | 1% | 0.5% |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Michigan's income tax rate?**
Michigan has a flat 4.25% state rate, plus local city taxes in some areas.

**What is Detroit's income tax?**
Detroit residents pay 2.4% city tax; non-residents working in Detroit pay 1.2%.
