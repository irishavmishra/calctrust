## Deep Dive: Wisconsin Tax Structure

### Wisconsin Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 3.5% on income up to $14,320
- 4.4% on income $14,321 to $28,640
- 5.3% on income $28,641 to $315,310
- 7.65% on income over $315,310
