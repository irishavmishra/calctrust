## Deep Dive: Kentucky Tax Structure

### Kentucky's Low Flat Tax

Kentucky's 4% flat rate is among the lowest for flat-tax states:
- Lower than Illinois (4.95%), Colorado (4.4%), Utah (4.65%)
- Simple calculation without brackets

### Local Occupational Taxes

Several Kentucky cities impose occupational taxes:
- Louisville: 2.2% local occupational tax
- Lexington: 2.25% local occupational tax
- Other cities vary
