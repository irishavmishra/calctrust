## Deep Dive: Tennessee's Tax-Free Advantage

### Tennessee Has No Income Tax

Tennessee completely eliminated income tax on all sources:
- No tax on wages/salaries
- No tax on interest/dividends (Hall Tax ended 2021)
- No tax on investment income

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Tennessee have income tax on wages?**
No. Tennessee has no income tax on any income source.

**When did Tennessee eliminate its income tax?**
The Hall Tax (on investment income) was phased out completely in 2021. Tennessee never taxed wages.
