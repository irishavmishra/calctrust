## Deep Dive: Montana Tax Structure

### Montana Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 4.7% on income up to $20,500
- 5.9% on income over $20,500

### Montana vs. Wyoming

Wyoming has no income tax. A $75,000 earner saves ~$4,000 annually in Wyoming vs. Montana.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Montana's income tax rate?**
Montana has graduated rates: 4.7% up to $20,500, 5.9% above.

**Does Montana have sales tax?**
No. Montana is one of five states with no sales tax.
