## Deep Dive: West Virginia Tax Structure

### West Virginia Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 2.36% on income up to $10,000
- 3.15% on income $10,001 to $25,000
- 3.54% on income $25,001 to $40,000
- 4.72% on income $40,001 to $60,000
- 5.12% on income over $60,000
