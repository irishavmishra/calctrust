## Deep Dive: New Mexico Tax Structure

### New Mexico Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 1.7% on income up to $5,500
- 3.2% on income $5,501 to $11,000
- 4.7% on income $11,001 to $16,000
- 4.9% on income $16,001 to $210,000
- 5.9% on income over $210,000

### New Mexico vs. Texas

Texas has no income tax. A $70,000 earner saves ~$3,000 annually in Texas vs. NM.
