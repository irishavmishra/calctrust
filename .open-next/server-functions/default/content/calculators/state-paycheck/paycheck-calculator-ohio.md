## Deep Dive: Ohio Tax Structure

### Ohio Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 0% on income up to $26,050
- 2.765% on income $26,051 to $100,000
- 3.5% on income $100,001 to $115,300
- 3.99% on income over $115,300

### Ohio City Taxes

Many Ohio cities impose local income taxes:

| City | Rate |
|------|------|
| Cleveland | 2.5% |
| Cincinnati | 2.1% |
| Columbus | 2.5% |
| Toledo | 2.5% |
| Dayton | 2.5% |

### Combined Burden

State (up to 3.99%) + City (up to 2.5%) can total 6.5%+.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Ohio's income tax rate?**
Ohio has graduated rates from 0% to 3.99%, with no tax on income under ~$26K.

**Do I pay city tax in Ohio?**
Depends on where you live and work. Many cities impose 1-2.5% local tax.
