## Deep Dive: Missouri Tax Structure

### Missouri Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 2% on income up to $1,207
- 2.5% on income $1,208 to $2,414
- 3% on income $2,415 to $3,621
- 3.5% on income $3,622 to $4,828
- 4% on income $4,829 to $6,035
- 4.5% on income $6,036 to $7,242
- 4.95% on income over $7,242

### Missouri vs. Kansas

Kansas City straddles both states. Missouri's 4.95% top rate is lower than Kansas's 5.7%.
