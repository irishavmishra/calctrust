## How a 3% Raise Works

A 3% raise increases your salary by 3/100ths. On a $50,000 salary, a 3% raise means $1,500 more per year, or $125 extra per month.

## FAQ

**How much is a 3% raise on $50,000?**
3% of $50,000 = $1,500/year → New salary: $51,500

**Is a 3% raise good?**
3% is considered a standard cost-of-living adjustment that roughly keeps pace with historical inflation. It's typical for annual merit increases.
