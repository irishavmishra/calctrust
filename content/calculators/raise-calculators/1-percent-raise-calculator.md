## How a 1% Raise Works

A 1% raise increases your salary by 1/100th of the current amount. On a $50,000 salary, a 1% raise means $500 more per year, or about $42 extra per month.

## FAQ

**How much is a 1% raise on $50,000?**
1% of $50,000 = $500/year increase → New salary: $50,500

**Is a 1% raise good?**
A 1% raise typically doesn't keep pace with average inflation (2-3%), so it may represent a real-wage decrease. Whether it's acceptable depends on your situation.
