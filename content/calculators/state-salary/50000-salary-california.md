## FAQ

**What is $50,000 after taxes in California?**
On $50,000 in California, you might take home approximately $38,500-$40,000 after federal and state taxes, depending on filing status and deductions.

**Why is California tax so high?**
California has a progressive income tax with rates from 1-13.3%, plus SDI at 1.1%. Combined with federal tax, effective rates can exceed 25-30%.
