## Deep Dive: North Dakota Tax Structure

### North Dakota Low Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 0% on income up to $44,725 (single)
- 1.95% on income $44,726 to $225,975
- 2.5% on income over $225,975

Most ND workers pay near-zero state tax.

### ND vs. Minnesota

Minnesota's top rate is 9.85%. A $75,000 earner saves ~$4,000 annually in ND vs. MN.
