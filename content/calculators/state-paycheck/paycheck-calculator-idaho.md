## How the Calculation Works

1. **Start with gross pay**: Total earnings before deductions
2. **Subtract pre-tax deductions**: 401(k), health insurance, HSA/FSA
3. **Calculate federal income tax**: Apply IRS brackets
4. **Calculate Idaho state tax**: Apply flat 5.8% rate
5. **Compute FICA**: Social Security (6.2%) and Medicare (1.45%)
6. **Arrive at net pay**: Your actual take-home

## Deep Dive: Idaho Tax Structure

### Idaho's Flat Tax

Idaho simplified to a flat 5.8% income tax rate in 2023, replacing the previous graduated system.

### Idaho vs. Neighboring States

| State | Tax Rate | Type |
|-------|----------|------|
| Washington | 0% | No income tax |
| Wyoming | 0% | No income tax |
| Idaho | 5.8% | Flat |
| Oregon | 9.9% | Graduated (high) |
| Montana | 5.9% | Graduated |

Idaho falls in the middle—more than no-tax neighbors but far less than Oregon.
