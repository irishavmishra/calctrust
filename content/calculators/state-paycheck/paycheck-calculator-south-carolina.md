## Deep Dive: South Carolina Tax Structure

### South Carolina Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 0% on income up to $3,460
- 3% on income $3,461 to $17,330
- 6.4% on income over $17,330

### SC vs. Neighbors

| State | Rate | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| South Carolina | 6.4% | Graduated |
| North Carolina | 4.75% | Flat |
| Georgia | 5.49% | Flat |
