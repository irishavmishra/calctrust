## Deep Dive: Nevada's Tax-Free Advantage

### Why Nevada Has No Income Tax

Nevada funds government through:
- Gaming and entertainment taxes
- Sales tax (6.85% state + local additions)
- Tourism and hotel taxes
- Mining industry taxes

### Nevada vs. California

California has the highest income tax (13.3%). A $100,000 earner saves ~$5,500 annually living in Nevada vs. California.
