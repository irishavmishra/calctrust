## Deep Dive: Maryland Tax Structure

### Maryland Graduated Rates

**State Rates (2025)**:
- 2% on income up to $1,000
- 3% on income $1,001 to $2,000
- 4% on income $2,001 to $3,000
- 4.75% on income $3,001 to $100,000
- 5% on income $100,001 to $125,000
- 5.25% on income $125,001 to $150,000
- 5.5% on income $150,001 to $250,000
- 5.75% on income over $250,000

### County Income Taxes

All Maryland counties impose local income taxes:

| County | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Montgomery | 3.2% |
| Prince George's | 3.2% |
| Baltimore County | 2.83% |
| Howard | 3.2% |
| Anne Arundel | 2.81% |

Combined state + county can exceed 8.5% for high earners.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Maryland's income tax rate?**
Maryland has state rates from 2% to 5.75%, plus county taxes from 2.25% to 3.2%.

**Do I have to pay county tax?**
Yes. Every Maryland resident pays county income tax based on residence county.
