## Deep Dive: New Hampshire's Tax Structure

### No Tax on Wages

New Hampshire doesn't tax wages, salaries, or other earned income. The state does tax interest and dividends at 4% (phasing out completely by 2027).

### New Hampshire vs. Massachusetts

Massachusetts has a flat 5% income tax. A $100,000 earner saves ~$5,000 annually living in NH vs. MA.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does New Hampshire have income tax on wages?**
No. New Hampshire has no income tax on wages, salaries, or earned income.

**What about the interest and dividends tax?**
NH taxes interest and dividends at 4%, but this phases out by 2027.
