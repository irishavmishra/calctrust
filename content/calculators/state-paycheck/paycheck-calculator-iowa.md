## Deep Dive: Iowa Tax Structure

### Iowa's Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 4.4% on income up to $6,210
- 4.82% on income $6,211 to $31,050
- 5.7% on income $31,051 to $77,625
- 6% on income over $77,625

### Federal Tax Deductibility

Iowa is one of only three states (with Alabama and Louisiana) allowing federal tax deduction from state taxable income, reducing effective state burden.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Iowa's income tax rate?**
Iowa has graduated rates from 4.4% to 6%, with the top rate on income over $77,625.

**Can I deduct federal taxes in Iowa?**
Yes. Iowa allows taxpayers to deduct federal income taxes paid from their Iowa taxable income.
