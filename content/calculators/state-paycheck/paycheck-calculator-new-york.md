## Deep Dive: New York Tax Structure

### New York State Rates

**2025 Rates (Single)**:
- 4% on income up to $8,500
- 4.5% on income $8,501 to $11,700
- 5.25% on income $11,701 to $13,900
- 5.5% on income $13,901 to $80,650
- 6% on income $80,651 to $215,400
- 6.85% on income $215,401 to $1,077,550
- 9.65% on income $1,077,551 to $5,000,000
- 10.3% on income $5,000,001 to $25,000,000
- 10.9% on income over $25,000,000

### NYC City Tax

NYC residents pay additional city tax on top of state:
- 3.078% to 3.876% depending on income

### Combined NYC Burden

High-earning NYC residents face combined 12-14%+ in state and city income taxes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is New York's income tax rate?**
New York state has graduated rates from 4% to 10.9%. NYC adds 3.1-3.9%.

**Do I pay NYC tax if I work in NYC but live elsewhere?**
No. NYC tax applies only to NYC residents, not commuters.
