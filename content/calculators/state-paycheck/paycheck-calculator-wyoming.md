## Deep Dive: Wyoming's Tax-Free Advantage

### Wyoming Has No Income Tax

Wyoming has no state income tax on any type of income. The state funds operations through:
- Mineral severance taxes (coal, oil, gas)
- Sales tax (4% state + local)
- Property taxes
- Tourism taxes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Wyoming have state income tax?**
No. Wyoming has no state income tax on any income.

**How does WY compare to Colorado?**
CO has a 4.4% flat tax. A $75,000 earner saves ~$3,300/year living in WY vs. CO.

**Why doesn't Wyoming have income tax?**
Wyoming funds state government primarily through mineral extraction taxes on coal, oil, and natural gas.
