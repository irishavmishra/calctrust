3. **Calculate federal income tax**: Apply IRS brackets

5. **Add local taxes if applicable**: Chicago and some localities have additional taxes

## Deep Dive: Illinois Tax Structure

### Illinois Flat Tax

Illinois maintains a straightforward 4.95% flat income tax rate:
- Entry-level worker: 4.95%
- Mid-career professional: 4.95%
- Executive: 4.95%

No bracket optimization needed—all income taxed equally.

### Local Taxes in Illinois

Unlike most states, Illinois has no local income taxes, but:
- Chicago has high sales and property taxes
- Cook County has additional taxes on various transactions

### Illinois vs. Neighboring States

| State | Tax Rate | Type |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Illinois's income tax rate?**
Illinois has a flat 4.95% income tax rate for all taxpayers.

No, Illinois has no local income taxes, though Chicago has high sales and property taxes.

**Is retirement income taxed in Illinois?**
No. Illinois doesn't tax Social Security, pensions, or retirement distributions.
