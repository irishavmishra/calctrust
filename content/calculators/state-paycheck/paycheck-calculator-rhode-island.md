## Deep Dive: Rhode Island Tax Structure

### Rhode Island Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 3.75% on income up to $73,450
- 4.75% on income $73,451 to $166,950
- 5.99% on income over $166,950

### RI vs. Massachusetts

MA has a flat 5% rate (plus 4% above $1M). RI's lower brackets can favor lower/middle income.
