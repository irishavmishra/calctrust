## Deep Dive: Oklahoma Tax Structure

### Oklahoma Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 0.25% on income up to $1,000
- 0.75% on income $1,001 to $2,500
- 1.75% on income $2,501 to $3,750
- 2.75% on income $3,751 to $4,900
- 3.75% on income $4,901 to $7,200
- 4.75% on income over $7,200

### Oklahoma vs. Texas

Texas has no income tax. A $70,000 earner saves ~$3,000 annually in Texas.
