## Deep Dive: New Jersey Tax Structure

### New Jersey Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 1.4% on income up to $20,000
- 1.75% on income $20,001 to $35,000
- 3.5% on income $35,001 to $40,000
- 5.525% on income $40,001 to $75,000
- 6.37% on income $75,001 to $500,000
- 8.97% on income $500,001 to $1,000,000
- 10.75% on income over $1,000,000

### NJ vs. NYC

NYC residents face combined state + city taxes of ~12-14%. NJ's max 10.75% can actually be lower for high earners.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is New Jersey's income tax rate?**
New Jersey has graduated rates from 1.4% to 10.75%, with the top rate applying to income over $1 million.

**Is NJ or NYC better for taxes?**
For very high earners, NJ's 10.75% max can beat NYC's combined 12-14% burden.
