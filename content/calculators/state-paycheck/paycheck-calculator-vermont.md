## Deep Dive: Vermont Tax Structure

### Vermont Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 3.35% on income up to $45,400
- 6.6% on income $45,401 to $110,050
- 7.6% on income $110,051 to $229,550
- 8.75% on income over $229,550

### Vermont vs. New Hampshire

NH has no income tax on wages. A $75,000 VT earner saves ~$4,500 living in NH.
