## Deep Dive: Virginia Tax Structure

### Virginia Graduated Rates

**2025 Rates**:
- 2% on income up to $3,000
- 3% on income $3,001 to $5,000
- 5% on income $5,001 to $17,000
- 5.75% on income over $17,000

### Virginia vs. Maryland

| State | Top Rate | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Virginia | 5.75% | No local income tax |
| Maryland | 5.75% | Plus 2.25-3.2% county tax |
| DC | 10.75% | High for high earners |

Virginia often beats Maryland due to no local income taxes.
