## Deep Dive: Georgia Tax Structure

### Georgia's Flat Tax System

Georgia implemented a flat tax structure in 2024:

- **Current rate (2025)**: 5.49%
- **2026 rate**: 5.39%
- **2027 rate**: 5.29%
- **2028 rate**: 5.19%
- **2029+ rate**: 4.99%

This progressive reduction makes Georgia increasingly competitive with neighboring states.

### Georgia vs. Neighboring States

| State | Tax Rate | Type |
|-------|----------|------|
| Florida | 0% | No income tax |
| Tennessee | 0% | No income tax |
| Georgia | 5.49% | Flat (declining) |
| Alabama | 5% | Graduated |
| South Carolina | 6.4% | Graduated |

### No Local Income Taxes

Georgia has no local income taxes—unlike some Ohio and Pennsylvania localities.
