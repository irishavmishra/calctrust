## Deep Dive: Delaware Tax Structure

### Delaware's Graduated Tax Rates

**All Filers**:
- 2.2% on income $2,001 to $5,000
- 3.9% on income $5,001 to $10,000
- 4.8% on income $10,001 to $20,000
- 5.2% on income $20,001 to $25,000
- 5.55% on income $25,001 to $60,000
- 6.6% on income over $60,000

### Delaware vs. Neighboring States

| State | Top Tax Rate | Notable Feature |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|
| Delaware | 6.6% | No sales tax |
| Pennsylvania | 3.07% | Flat rate, local taxes |
| New Jersey | 10.75% | High top rate |
| Maryland | 5.75% | Plus local taxes |

Delaware's lack of sales tax offsets the moderate income tax for some residents.

### No Local Income Tax

Delaware has no local income taxes—unlike neighboring Philadelphia's 3.75% city tax.
